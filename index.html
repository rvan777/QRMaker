About TBHQR

TBHQR is a free, open, privacy-first QR code generator built for people who care about their data.

Our Mission

Most online QR generators silently log every URL, Wi-Fi password, and contact card you type into them. We think that's wrong. TBHQR proves you can have a beautiful, fully-featured QR tool that never sees your data.

How It Works

Every QR code is generated entirely inside your browser using JavaScript. When you type your Wi-Fi password or contact details, that information stays on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server. You can verify this by:

  1. Opening your browser's Network tab (F12) and confirming no requests fire while you generate codes.
  2. Disconnecting from the internet after the page loads — TBHQR will continue to work perfectly.

Features

  • 7 data types: URL, Text, Wi-Fi, vCard, Email, SMS, Geo-location
  • Full visual customization: colors, shapes, corners, dots
  • Optional logo embedding (processed locally)
  • Custom captions above and below the code
  • Export as PNG, SVG, JPG, or PDF
  • Works offline after first load

Built With

TBHQR is a static web application that runs on any free hosting platform (Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, GitHub Pages, Vercel). There is no backend — by design.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

The Short Version

We do not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share any data about you or the QR codes you create. Full stop.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ We do not run analytics that capture your inputs
  • ❌ We do not use third-party trackers, pixels, or fingerprinting
  • ❌ We do not set advertising cookies
  • ❌ We do not have a database — there's nowhere to store your data even if we wanted to
  • ❌ We do not require accounts, sign-ups, or email addresses
  • ❌ We do not send any QR content to a server

How Your Data Is Handled

All QR code generation happens locally in your browser. The URLs, Wi-Fi passwords, contact details, and any other content you enter are processed by JavaScript running on your own device and discarded the moment you close the tab.

Logo Uploads

If you upload a logo image, it is read into your browser's memory using the standard FileReader API. The image is never uploaded to any server. It exists only in the tab you have open.

Hosting & Third Parties

This site is served as static files from a content delivery network. Your hosting provider may log standard request metadata (IP address, user agent) for abuse prevention, as is standard for any website. We do not access or process those logs.

We load two open-source JavaScript libraries from a public CDN (jsDelivr) to render QR codes and PDFs. These are static asset requests and do not transmit any QR content.

Cookies & Local Storage

TBHQR does not set any cookies and does not write to localStorage or sessionStorage.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. You are already in full control.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated date above will reflect it. Our commitment to zero data collection will not change.
